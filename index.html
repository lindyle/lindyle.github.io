Hi! I'm Lindy.

I'm passionate about fostering compassion and connection.

Within Human-Computer Interaction, my research interests are neurodiversity, empathy, and accessibility. I'm currently a product manager at Microsoft, where I built assistive technology for people with visual, mobility, and cognitive disabilities. I also collaborate with Microsoft Research's Human Understanding and Empathy group, researching technologies to promote emotional resilience and well-being. My approach to research is transdisciplinary: I draw on my experience in computer science, neuroscience, psychology, communications, sociology, and philosophy. In my free time, I love making art through needlework (e.g. knitting, crochet), music (e.g. ocarina, ukuele), and dance (e.g. aerial silks, ballroom).
